At Grape Pirates, we know that good things take time!

At Grape Pirates, we believe in letting nature take the lead. Our philosophy is simple: allow the grapes to tell their own story, capturing the essence of each vintage with minimal intervention. This approach results in wines that truly reflect their origin and season.

Nestled in the cool climate wine region of Orange, NSW, our small vineyard sits at an impressive 900 meters above sea level. Here, Bex and Aaron, the passionate duo behind Grape Pirates, nurture their vines using sustainable and organic practices. Their hands-on approach ensures that each grape receives the care it deserves.

While our vineyard is the heart of our operation, we also collaborate with local growers to source additional grape varieties, expanding our palette of flavors. Aaron, our resident "wine pirate," crafts our wines at a nearby winery, overseeing the journey from fruit to bottle with meticulous attention to detail.
